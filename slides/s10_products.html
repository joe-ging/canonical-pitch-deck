10. The Charmed Stack

Architectural Blueprint: Metal to Cloud

1. Infrastructure Layer (MAAS)

"Bare Metal as a Service"

  • Automated provisioning of 5,000+ servers.
  • IPAM, DHCP, and PXE boot management.
  • Inventory control (CPU, RAM, Disk, NIC).

2. Storage Layer (Ceph)

"Software Defined Storage"

  • Scalable Object & Block storage for 5PB+.
  • Self-healing, no single point of failure.
  • Reuses commodity hardware for high performance.

3. Network & Compute (OpenStack)

"The Cloud Engine"

  • KVM: High-performance virtualization.
  • OVN/OVS: Software Defined Networking.
  • Multi-tenancy and resource quotas.

4. Operations Layer (Juju)

"Model-Driven Automation"

  • Encapsulates operational code into "Charms".
  • Day-0 to Day-2 lifecycle management.
  • Scale, upgrade, and integrate with one command.
Macro View: We don't just sell software; we provide a consistent operational model. Whether it's 10 nodes or 10,000 nodes, the management logic remains identical through Juju and MAAS.
