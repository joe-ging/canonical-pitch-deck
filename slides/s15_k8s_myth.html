14. The Real Cost Drivers

"Can't We Just Use K8s on AWS?"

The Theory

Deploy Kubernetes on AWS. Auto-scale down at night. Only pay for what you use.

Sounds logical. But it doesn't work.

The Reality

AWS On-Demand pricing is 2.5-3x more expensive per hour than Reserved Instances.

Scale down 60% × Pay 2.7x more per unit = you save almost nothing.

But that's not even the main problem.

K8s optimizes compute. But your two biggest cost drivers — Egress and Storage — are completely unaffected by container orchestration.

Students still watch videos. 1.5 PB/month still flows out. AWS still charges $0.09/GB.
