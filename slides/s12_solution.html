11. The Proposal

Hybrid Cloud — Not "Replace AWS"

Our Recommendation

We are not asking you to abandon AWS.

We recommend a hybrid architecture where:

  • 88% of workload → Canonical Private Cloud (predictable, sovereign, high-egress)
  • 12% of workload → AWS auto-scaling burst (unpredictable spikes only)

Result: You keep AWS as your "safety airbag", not your primary bill.

Placement Principle→ Private→ Public
